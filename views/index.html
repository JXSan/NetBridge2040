About Us

Our mission is to bridge the gap between minorities and the tech industry. We do this by providing a platform that connects minorities with professionals who resemble certain characteristics they posess. A recent survey of the top 9 tech companies in Silicon Valley by Fortune reveals that on average, minorities comprise about one-third of the workforce. That gap expands the higher up you go in an organization, with the best company showing women holding 29% of leadership jobs. NetBridge aims to solve that problem.
